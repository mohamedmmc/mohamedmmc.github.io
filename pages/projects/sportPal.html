SportPal

Sports matching app to find partners and organize tournaments

Year

2022

Technology used

Dart Flutter Node.js

ABOUT THE PROJECT

The general idea was to find a solution for those who want to practice any type of sport without having a partner. Basically it's a matching app, where you'll have to create an account in order to see other profiles according to the favorite sport selected, and after that you'll have to swipe left or right to send requests for matches.

Tournament can be also created with winning prizes
