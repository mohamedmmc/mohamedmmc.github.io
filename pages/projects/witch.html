ABOUT THE PROJECT

Once upon a time, in a very far away village, a witch terrified the poor villagers and made their places a burning hell. And from the dust, a hero was born, he was called « FLEN » and known as the most brave man the villagers counted. He decided to get his revenge from the witch.
