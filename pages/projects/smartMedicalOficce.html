ABOUT THE PROJECT

Smart Medical Office is an application that allows users (employees) to keep confidential information of the patient and even the administration safe in order to guarantee customer satisfaction and provide better service to its patients. This platform is simple and easy to use. It contains all the integrated data in an organized way, hence the speed of service to patients. Presentation of the theme
