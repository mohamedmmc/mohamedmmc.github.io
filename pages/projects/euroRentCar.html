Year

2020

Technology used

“EuroRent A Car” developed in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and PhP using WAMPServe

ABOUT THE PROJECT

EuroRent A Car aims to create a fast and efficient management of the agency, and to offer a reliable and easy-to-use site for customers. Anonymous user, can browse the website to consult the services offered. Customer, who can buy one or more products after creating an account. Agents, who manage the addition of new products, the management of reservations, drivers, users as well as the management of promotions. Administrator, who has all the features of agents but can also manage leave requests.
